1. Always backup your savegame located at (%userprofile%\appdata\locallow\Red Dot Games)
2. Navigate to your Steam CMS2018 folder, usually C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\Car Mechanic Simulator 2018\cms2018_Data\Managed
3. Make a copy of "Assembly-CSharp-firstpass.dll" in the above folder, just in case
4. Copy contents from the .zip to cms21018_Data\Managed
5. Open game and wait for a short period as the intro videos are removed

Change baku.ini settings as required.

Bonus: 
- No intro videos
- Always 23 cars in junkyard
- Double walking speed by default
